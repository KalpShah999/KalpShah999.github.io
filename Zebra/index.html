Need For Speed: Most Wanted (2012)

A blog by Kalp Shah - July 28, 2022

"Need For Speed: Most Wanted (2012)" is by far my favourite game ever.
With its amazing graphics, fluid controls, and enticing gameplay, it very quickly has become my go to game for any mood.



What is Need For Speed: Most Wanted?

On October 30, 2012, Electronic Arts released a remake of their earlier game "Need For Speed: Most Wanted", called "Need For Speed: Most Wanted (2012)." Most Wanted is a street racing game set in the fictional town called "Fairhaven City". With illegal street racing filling the streets, the player seeks to challenge as many drivers around the city to gain repution, and become the city's most wanted. Although the name of the game didn't change much, it's updated gameplay and graphics made a huge impact.

The Wormhole of Free Racing

With over 60+ unique races to be won with 45+ different cars, and unlimited online competition, players can spend hours upon hours within the game. Some other things to collect include:

  • Smash through 156 billboards
  • Collect all 123 car jackspots
  • Speed past 66 speed cameras
  • Takedown all 10 most wanted cars
  • Run away from the heated car chases
